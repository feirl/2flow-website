What the hub handles

Built for Irish eCommerce brands

The Dublin hub is set up specifically for DTC and multi-channel brands selling across Ireland, the UK and the EU. Everything from ambient and temperature-controlled storage to custom kitting and branded packaging.

Same-day dispatch

Orders placed before 1pm dispatch the same day. The cut-off is firm, the process is built around it, and the team runs to it every day.

Multi-brand storage

Multiple brands, multiple SKUs — all managed separately on the same floor. Dedicated racking, clear labelling, no cross-contamination between client stock.

Temperature-controlled storage

Refrigerated units for health, wellness and food products requiring cold chain storage. One of the few Dublin 3PLs set up to handle it.

Pick accuracy

Every pick barcode-scanned against the order. The system validates product, quantity and destination before anything leaves the shelf.

Custom kitting & gift packaging

Bundle assembly, gift wrapping, branded inserts, custom tissue and ribbons. Kitting configured per brand to spec, handled consistently at volume.

27 EU destinations

Shipping to 27 EU countries from Dublin, plus the UK and worldwide. Carrier routing is automatic — the right service for each destination, every time.

Laser engraving

On-site laser engraving for bottle caps, product lids and personalised items. A specialist capability handled in the same building as your stock — no handoff to a third party, no extra transit.
