About 2Flow

2Flow is a Dublin-based third-party logistics (3PL) and eCommerce fulfilment provider. We store, pick, pack and dispatch orders for Irish DTC and multi-channel brands, integrating directly with Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop and other platforms.

2Flow works with Irish and international eCommerce brands selling DTC and across multiple channels. We have particular experience in health, beauty, wellness, lifestyle and fragrance. Brands typically have between 100 and several thousand orders per month.

2Flow operates from a fulfilment hub in Dublin, Ireland. All orders are dispatched from Dublin to Ireland, the UK and 27 EU countries.

Book a call with the team. They will assess your volume, SKU count and requirements and come back with a proposal. Most brands are fully onboarded within a few weeks.
