Full capability list

Everything the Shopify integration does

01

Automatic order import

Every Shopify order syncs to our warehouse system automatically. No manual exports, no CSV files, no scheduled imports. Orders arrive continuously throughout the day.

02

Order validation and rules

Orders are validated on import against your product catalogue. Custom rules can be configured to route, flag, or hold orders based on conditions you define - address format, product combinations, order value.

03

Automatic carrier selection

The system selects the right carrier and service level based on destination, weight and your routing rules. Labels are generated automatically. No manual carrier decisions per order.

04

Tracking number pushback

As soon as an order is dispatched, the tracking number is pushed back to Shopify and the order is marked fulfilled. Your customer receives their dispatch confirmation without anyone on your team intervening.

05

Real-time inventory sync

Stock levels in Shopify are updated in real time as items are picked, dispatched and returned. What your store shows is what is actually in the warehouse - preventing overselling and keeping your catalogue accurate.

06

Multi-storefront support

Running Shopify Markets, multiple storefronts or selling across currencies? The integration handles routing across storefronts and currency contexts without needing separate connections.

07

Product catalogue sync

Products added or updated in Shopify can be reflected in the warehouse system. SKU data, variants, weights and dimensions stay aligned between your store and our operation.

08

Returns processing

Returned items are received, inspected and logged back into stock through the same system. Returns are visible in the client portal and stock adjustments flow back to Shopify when items are restocked.
