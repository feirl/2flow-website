The switch

After

Several years in. The brand has grown significantly and sells internationally. The founder describes 2Flow not as a vendor but as a partner - the kind where, when something unusual comes up, he can reach someone he knows and get it sorted. He has turned up unannounced with an urgent request and been accommodated. He recommends 2Flow openly in his ecommerce peer network, without hesitation.

"When you've got an issue, you like to know that there's a human whose name you recognise who can say, 'I know it's important to you, I can get this sorted.' The 2Flow guys have always done that."

"They've always tried their hardest. Sometimes we make mistakes, sometimes they make mistakes - but it's the integrity. It feels like a partner."

Story 04

Luxury Fragrance Brand  ·  Country Manager

Dangerous goods. Glass bottles.
When trust is the only thing that matters.

The operation

The brand is a niche luxury fragrance house, distributed globally. The Irish operation handles products classified as dangerous goods - alcohol-based fragrances in glass bottles, limited-quantity shipping, routes to the US, Middle East, and across Europe. Getting this wrong isn't just a customer service problem. It carries compliance implications. When the brand decided to expand this side of the operation, the founder came to visit 2Flow personally before committing.

Three years in

The relationship is built entirely on trust. The country manager has real-time stock visibility through the platform at any moment - a capability the brand doesn't have with its other operations, where stock is still counted manually. Response times from the 2Flow team average under an hour. 2Flow also handles laser engraving for bottle caps - a specialist value-add that required custom setup. When a carrier issue emerged, the team worked through a solution proactively rather than waiting to be pushed.

"Trust is huge. You're trusting a company with all of your stock, all of your growth, and the whole team there."

"Trust in the sense that you know your stock is safe there. And trust that you know they're going to do what you ask them to do - no questions asked, just get on with it."

"I'm never waiting any longer than an hour to get an answer back from them."
