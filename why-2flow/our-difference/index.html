Common questions

What brands ask before switching

What makes 2Flow different from other 3PLs?
2Flow takes full ownership of the fulfilment chain, not just picking and packing. Brands consistently describe the same experience: they stop thinking about fulfilment. Orders go out, tracking comes back, stock is visible in real time. No chasing, no surprises, no managing the relationship.
Does 2Flow work for brands that are scaling fast?
Yes. 2Flow is designed to scale with you without renegotiation. Brands have grown from 600 to 4,000 orders per month without changing any part of their fulfilment setup. The same operation handles both ends of that range.
What is the 2Flow client portal?
The 2Flow client portal is powered by Mintsoft WMS and gives you real-time visibility into stock levels, order status, dispatch history and returns. You no longer need to email your 3PL to find out what stock you have. Everything is live and always accessible.
How does 2Flow handle problems when they arise?
2Flow operates as a partner, not a vendor. When issues arise, the team is typically already on it before the brand even knows. One point of contact, one point of accountability, no finger-pointing between systems or carriers.
How quickly can we get started with 2Flow?
Most brands are operational within a few weeks of contracting. The process starts with a call to understand your volumes, product types and platform integrations. From there, 2Flow handles the setup, Shopify connection and inbound stock process. You don't need to be technical to get started.
