The Category Challenge

What makes beauty fulfilment different

Beauty and personal care isn't just high-volume eCommerce. It's high-stakes eCommerce. The margin for error is tighter, the presentation expectations are higher, and the consequences of getting it wrong are more visible to your customer than in almost any other category.

Similar-looking SKUs, high-value contents

Picking the wrong 200ml bottle for a €150 serum is a costly mistake. For your brand and your customer. Beauty ranges have tighter margins for picking errors than almost any other category, with packaging so similar that precision picking can't be assumed. It has to be built in.

The unboxing is part of the product

Your packaging, tissue, inserts, and presentation are not secondary details. They are how your brand lands at the doorstep. For a generic 3PL, a box is a box. For your customers. Especially for premium skincare and fragrance. The opening experience is the first moment your product promise is either kept or broken.

Peaks that arrive without warning

Black Friday. Valentine's Day. Mother's Day. A viral moment. A product launch. Each one can double or triple your order volume overnight. Beauty brands live and die by their ability to turn peak moments into peak revenue. Which means your fulfilment partner needs to scale with you, not buckle under you.

Fragile Goods Handling

Glass bottles, liquid cosmetics, blast-packed products. 2Flow handles the full range with strict packing protocols and low breakage across glass and liquid SKUs. Beauty brands ship fragile cosmetics to four continents through us.

Alcohol-based fragrances and aerosols require specialist handling and carrier classification. 2Flow is one of the only Irish fulfilment partners with operational dangerous goods capability. Essential for fragrance brands.
