The Category Challenge

What makes healthcare fulfilment different

Healthcare isn't just regulated eCommerce. It's a category where a fulfilment error has consequences beyond a bad review. Traceability, compliance, and precision aren't differentiators in this space. They're the entry requirement.

Traceability is a regulatory requirement, not a feature

Healthcare products must be traceable from manufacturer to end user. Lot numbers, batch codes, and expiry dates need to be logged at every stage. A 3PL that can't provide this isn't just inconvenient. It may make your supply chain non-compliant.

Returns need to be managed, not just processed

Healthcare returns can't go straight back to sellable stock. Products need to be inspected, quarantined if necessary, and documented before any reinstatement. Generic returns processing creates serious risk in this category.

Labelling requirements vary by market and product class

CE marking, MDR compliance, country-specific labelling. Healthcare products often need market-specific label variations. Without in-house re-labelling capability, you're either maintaining separate SKUs per market or accepting non-compliance risk.
