The problem

Why Irish fulfilment is harder than it looks

It's a small market but not a simple one. Single-carrier strategies fail. Multi-channel demand is real. And customer expectations are set by the best delivery they've ever had, not the average.

Single-carrier strategies fail

DPD is fast but costs more in rural areas. An Post covers every townland but takes longer into metro. Cyclone is brilliant for same-day Dublin but doesn't leave the county. You need all three, routed intelligently per shipment.

Customers expect choice at checkout

Next-day, economy, same-day, click-and-collect, pickup points. Each option converts a different customer. Offering only one is leaving conversions on the table, offering all without thinking costs you margin.

Channels have different needs

Retail distribution is pallet logistics, EDI, and compliance-ready prep. Amazon is FBA prep and Seller Central. DTC is address-level routing and branded packing. One warehouse needs to do all three without dropping the ball on any.

Common questions

Ireland fulfilment questions answered

The questions Irish brands typically ask before switching to 2Flow.

Which carrier does 2Flow recommend for Ireland?
There's no single right answer. DPD Ireland is fast and reliable for most addresses. An Post has wider rural coverage and parcel lockers. Cyclone Couriers handles same-day Dublin. 2Flow selects the right carrier per shipment based on destination, weight and service level. You don't manage carrier accounts.
Can I offer multiple shipping options at checkout?
Yes. 2Flow can support next-day, economy 2-3 day, same-day Dublin, and click-and-collect options depending on your store platform. We also advise on which options to offer based on your customer base and average order value.
Do you handle deliveries to Northern Ireland?
Yes. Northern Ireland is covered through DPD and An Post's NI partner network. Post-Brexit arrangements mean NI shipments are treated effectively as EU-internal for most consumer goods, which simplifies the process versus mainland UK.
What is the dispatch cut-off for next-day Ireland delivery?
Standard next-day dispatch cut-off is 1pm. Orders received before 1pm are dispatched the same day for next-day arrival. Same-day Dublin delivery via Cyclone Couriers has a noon cut-off.
Do you deliver to parcel lockers or pickup points?
Yes. An Post parcel lockers and DPD pickup points are both supported. These are increasingly popular options for customers who aren't home during delivery hours and can reduce failed-delivery costs.
