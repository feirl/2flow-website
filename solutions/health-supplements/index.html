The Category Challenge

What makes supplement fulfilment different

Health supplements aren't generic eCommerce. Regulatory obligations, expiry risk, and compliance requirements mean the margin for error is tighter than almost any other category. And the consequences of getting it wrong go beyond a bad review.

Expiry and batch compliance is non-negotiable

Shipping an expired supplement isn't just a returns issue. It's a brand and regulatory risk. Without proper batch tracking and FIFO enforcement at the system level, expired stock will ship. It's not a matter of if, it's when.

Labelling requirements change by market

Supplement labelling for Ireland, the EU and the UK isn't identical. A product that's correctly labelled for one market may need re-labelling for another. Without a fulfilment partner who understands this, you're either over-risking or over-spending on separate SKUs.

Subscription volumes need precision at scale

Supplement brands live on repeat purchase. Subscription orders go out every week. And every single one needs to be right. A mispick on a supplement subscription doesn't just lose you one order, it can end the relationship.
