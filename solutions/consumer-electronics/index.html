The Category Challenge

What makes electronics fulfilment different

Consumer electronics isn't just high-volume eCommerce. It's high-liability eCommerce. The margin for picking errors is near zero, the compliance requirements are strict, and a single mismatch between a serial number and a dispatch note can cost far more than the product itself.

High-value SKUs with zero tolerance for error

Sending the wrong model, missing a serial number scan, or dispatching an unverified unit isn't a minor inconvenience. It's a warranty failure, a returns headache, and a customer trust problem. Electronics fulfilment requires a level of pick discipline that most 3PLs aren't built for.

Returns that need to be properly assessed

Electronics returns aren't just inbound stock. They're units that need to be powered on, tested, graded, and either returned to sell-through or flagged for the brand. A generic 3PL puts them in a box. 2Flow puts them through a process.

Battery & compliance rules that change by destination

Lithium batteries. UN38.3 certification. IATA restricted cargo classifications. The rules change depending on what you're shipping, how it's packed, and where it's going. Brands that get it wrong face carrier refusals and regulatory risk. 2Flow handles the classification so you don't have to.

What We Do For You

The services electronics brands use most

From standard DTC fulfilment to serialised inventory control and returns assessment, here's what 2Flow handles for consumer electronics clients every day.

Serialised Pick & Pack

Every unit scanned at pick and matched to its dispatch note. Serial numbers logged against order records. Giving brands and customers a full audit trail from warehouse to doorstep.

Returns Assessment & Processing

Inbound returns are powered on, tested, and graded against your condition matrix. Sellable units go back to available stock. Faulty units are flagged, photographed, and reported. Ready for warranty or supplier claims.

Battery & Dangerous Goods Compliance

Lithium-ion and lithium-polymer batteries require specialist carrier classification. 2Flow handles UN38.3, IATA, and ADR compliance so your shipments move without delays, refusals, or regulatory risk.

High-Value Goods Handling

Dedicated secure storage zones, controlled access, and handling protocols designed for premium and high-value electronics. No mixed pallets, no shared racking with low-value commodity stock.

Bundle & Accessory Kitting

Device + accessories + packaging assembled to spec. Whether that's a retail-ready bundle, a promotional gift set, or a subscription hardware kit. Consistent presentation on every unit.

International Shipping

DTC fulfilment across Ireland, the EU, UK, and rest of world. Carrier selection managed by 2Flow. Including battery-compliant routing for restricted destinations.
