The problem

What breaks when you scale without the right 3PL

Every DTC brand hits the same walls. The operation that worked at 200 orders a month stops working at 2,000. Here's where things typically fall apart.

Fulfilment is consuming founder time

Packing orders, chasing couriers, reconciling stock spreadsheets. The operations that once felt manageable now eat entire days, time that should be in product, marketing and acquisition.

Your current setup doesn't scale

A spare bedroom, a shared unit, or a generalist 3PL that wasn't built for your volumes. What worked at the start starts to crack, missed dispatches, stock errors, customer complaints, as order volumes grow.

Integration is a constant pain point

CSV exports, manual order imports, tracking numbers pasted into spreadsheets. Every gap between your Shopify store and your fulfilment operation is a potential error, and the errors compound with volume.

Common questions

DTC fulfilment questions answered

The things DTC founders typically ask before switching to 2Flow.

What size DTC brand does 2Flow work with?
2Flow is best suited to DTC brands shipping from around 500 orders per month upwards, through to 10,000+ orders per month. We're designed to scale with you: the same operation handles 500 orders and 10,000 orders without you needing to change partner or renegotiate contracts.
Does 2Flow integrate with Shopify?
Yes. 2Flow has a native Shopify integration through Mintsoft WMS. Orders sync within 15 minutes of being placed, tracking numbers are pushed back to Shopify automatically, and inventory levels update in real time. No CSV uploads, no manual intervention.
What is the same-day dispatch cut-off?
Orders received before 1pm are dispatched the same day. Tracking is automatically pushed back to Shopify once the order ships. For same-day delivery within Dublin city and county, the cut-off is noon via our partner Cyclone Couriers.
Which carriers does 2Flow use?
2Flow manages DPD Ireland and An Post on your behalf for standard domestic and international shipments. Carrier selection is handled by 2Flow based on destination, weight and service level, no carrier accounts for you to manage. For same-day Dublin delivery, we use Cyclone Couriers.
Can I see my stock levels and order status in real time?
Yes. 2Flow provides a real-time client portal through Mintsoft WMS where you can see live inventory levels, order status, dispatch history and returns. No more emailing your 3PL to find out what stock you have.
