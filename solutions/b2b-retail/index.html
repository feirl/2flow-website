The problem

Where retail suppliers lose money quietly

Retail chargebacks rarely come from one big failure. They come from the same small compliance gaps, repeated across months, that never quite get fixed because the ops team is too busy shipping.

Labels don't match retailer specs

SSCC formats, barcode placement, pallet cards, shelf-ready markings. Every retailer is slightly different, and the wrong label is an instant chargeback.

EDI and ASN are fragile

In-house EDI integrations break whenever the retailer changes a field. ASNs arrive late or with mismatches and the shipment gets refused at the DC gate.

Booking slots eat ops time

Each retailer has its own portal, lead time and cut-offs. Someone on your team is spending hours every week managing slots rather than growing the business.

How we handle it

Built for retailer compliance, not just order volume

2Flow's B2B operation was built around the demands of Irish and UK retail. Labels, ASNs, booking slots and paperwork are part of the standard process, not a bolt-on.

Retailer-specific labelling

SSCC, GS1-128, shelf-ready cases, pallet cards. Each retailer's spec is maintained centrally and applied at pick, so labels always match the destination DC.

EDI intake and ASN generation

Orders arrive over EDI. ASNs are generated, validated against the shipment and sent inside the retailer's lead time window. No manual files, no last-minute scrambles.

Booking slot management

We run the retailer portals on your behalf: book the slot, align the pick window, hand off to the carrier, and chase when something slips. You see the outcome, not the queue.

Case and pallet picking

Pick to eaches, cases, layers or full pallets from the same stock pool. Mixed pallets built to retailer specification. Shelf-ready and store-ready preparation where required.

One stock pool, two channels

The same SKUs can ship as DTC orders one hour and leave on a pallet to a retailer DC the next. No split inventory, no fragmented stock counts, no missed sales because the "wrong" pool ran out.

Compliance as a process, not a heroic effort

Pre-dispatch checks on labels, paperwork and ASN matching happen on every shipment, not just the big ones. Chargebacks fall because the error never leaves the site.

Common questions

B2B & retail fulfilment questions answered

The things retail suppliers typically want to understand before moving their operation.

Which Irish and UK retailers can 2Flow ship to?
2Flow ships to all major Irish and UK grocery, pharmacy and general retailers, including those operating their own DC networks and booking portals. We manage retailer-specific labelling, paperwork and booking slots so your stock lands compliantly.
Does 2Flow support EDI and ASN messaging?
Yes. 2Flow handles EDI order intake, Advance Shipping Notices and related retailer messaging. We map your SKUs to the retailer's catalogue and manage the data flow so you aren't building integrations in-house.
Can 2Flow run B2B and DTC from the same stock?
Yes. One stock pool, two dispatch flows. The same SKU can ship as a single DTC order in the morning and leave on a pallet to a retailer DC in the afternoon, with full stock visibility across both.
How do you reduce retailer chargebacks?
Chargebacks usually come from labelling errors, missed booking slots or incorrect paperwork. 2Flow's process is built around retailer compliance: SSCC and GS1 labelling, validated ASNs, and slot-managed dispatch, all checked before leaving the site.
Can 2Flow build mixed pallets and store-ready cases?
Yes. We pick to case and pallet, build mixed pallets to retailer specifications, and prepare shelf-ready and store-ready units where required.
