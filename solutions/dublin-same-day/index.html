The problem

Why scheduled carriers can't do same-day

Same-day isn't a faster version of next-day. It's a different operational model entirely. Most brands either can't offer it or offer it badly.

Scheduled carriers run on routes, not windows

DPD and An Post collect once a day, process overnight, and deliver tomorrow. That's the whole model. Same-day sits outside their operational design and they can't retrofit it cheaply.

In-house fleets are expensive to run

Vans, insurance, fuel, drivers, maintenance, routing software. A same-day fleet only pays back at serious volume. For most brands, it's not the right investment.

Some products genuinely need same-day

Medical products with a patient waiting. Cold-chain food. Urgent B2B replacements. Luxury items where next-day is simply off-brand. These aren't edge cases - they're specific customers who pay a premium for speed.

Common questions

Same-day Dublin questions answered

The things brands ask before adding same-day to their delivery options.

What's the cut-off time for same-day Dublin delivery?
Orders received before noon are delivered the same afternoon. That's a real cut-off, not a best-effort. Orders after noon go out on the next same-day run.
What areas are covered?
Dublin city and Dublin county are covered as standard. Cyclone also operates across much of greater Leinster for point-to-point deliveries. If you're not sure whether an address is in scope, 2Flow will check at the order stage.
Can you do temperature-controlled same-day delivery?
Yes. Cyclone already runs temperature-sensitive deliveries for medical and healthcare clients, including weight-loss medications packed in ice. Fridge-chain and cold-chain delivery is available for 2Flow clients as part of the same-day service.
Is there a minimum order volume for same-day Dublin?
No. Same-day Dublin is available on a per-order basis as part of your 2Flow fulfilment service. You can use it for one urgent order or for a steady flow. There's no minimum spend or commitment.
How is this different from DPD or An Post next-day?
DPD and An Post are scheduled carriers running fixed routes. Their earliest delivery for a Dublin order placed today is tomorrow. Same-day Dublin via Cyclone uses a dedicated point-to-point fleet that operates on same-day windows, not next-day routes.
