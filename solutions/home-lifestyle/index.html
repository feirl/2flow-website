The challenges

What home & lifestyle brands get wrong with fulfilment

Presentation, gifting peaks, and SKU complexity make this category harder to fulfil than most. Here is where things break.

Presentation is judged before the product is used

Dented corners, missing inserts, wrong tissue paper. In home and lifestyle, the packaging experience is the first product moment. A fulfilment partner without packaging standards will erode your brand before your customer even opens the box.

Gifting peaks are unpredictable and punishing

Christmas, Valentine's, Mother's Day, Black Friday. Lifestyle brands have multiple gifting peaks every year, each with compressed windows and higher-than-normal gift messaging and wrapping requirements. A warehouse that can't flex doesn't just miss a sale. It misses the season.

SKU proliferation makes inventory accuracy critical

Colour variants, size variants, seasonal lines, gift sets. Home and lifestyle catalogues grow fast. Without robust inventory management and accurate picking, the wrong item reaches the wrong customer. And in gifting, there's rarely a chance to recover.
