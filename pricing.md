## Pricing Model

2Flow does not publish a fixed rate card. Pricing is tailored and itemised for each brand.
A custom quote is delivered within a few working days of a discovery call.

## Quote Components

Quotes are built from six components:

### 1. Inbound
Receiving stock into the warehouse: unloading, inspection, putaway, ASN processing.
Charged per pallet / per consignment.

### 2. Storage
On-site storage of your stock at Finglas, Dublin.
Charged per pallet / per shelf / per cubic metre, billed weekly or monthly.
Refrigerated storage available for cold-chain SKUs (surcharge applies).

### 3. Pick and Pack
Core per-order fulfilment service.
Charged per order (base rate) + per unit line picked.
Includes standard branded packing. Custom packaging options quoted separately.

### 4. Value-Add Services
Optional services, quoted per task / per unit:
- Kitting and bundling (multi-SKU assembly, gift sets, subscription boxes)
- Laser engraving (bottle caps, lids, personalised items — in-house, no outsourcing)
- Custom inserts, tissue paper, ribbon, gift messaging
- Compliance labelling and re-labelling (EU / UK market-specific)
- Amazon FBA prep (labelling, poly-bagging, carton configuration)

### 5. Shipping
Carrier costs passed through to the brand at 2Flow's carrier rates.
2Flow manages carrier selection (DPD Ireland, An Post, DHL, FedEx, UPS).
Same-day Dublin delivery via Cyclone Couriers (additional rate applies, no minimum volume).

### 6. Returns
Per return processed: receipt, inspection, grading, restocking or quarantine.
Tracked in real time through client portal.

## What's Included as Standard

- Onboarding and integration setup (Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, TikTok Shop)
- Real-time client portal (inventory, order tracking, inbound ASN, reports, low stock alerts)
- Named account team (no helpdesk)
- Carrier management and rate optimisation

---

## Minimum Volume

No published minimum order volume.
Service is optimised for brands shipping approximately **500+ orders per month**.
Same-day Dublin delivery (via Cyclone Couriers) has no minimum order volume.
