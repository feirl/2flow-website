For couriers & delivery drivers

Delivering to 2Flow

We receive inbound stock Monday to Friday between 09:00 and 16:30. Booking ahead speeds everything up.

  • Pallet deliveries: please book a slot in advance via inbound@2flow.ie
  • Parcel couriers: front reception, signed for during office hours
  • Driver facilities available on site
  • Trailer access and turning space for HGVs
  • Reference your PO or ASN number on arrival
