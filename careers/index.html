Build your career in Irish fulfilment

We're growing 2Flow from our Dublin hub. If you care about operational excellence, brand-aligned fulfilment, and doing right by the customer, we'd love to hear from you.

Why 2Flow

2Flow is Ireland's specialist eCommerce fulfilment partner. We pick, pack, kit, label, refurbish and deliver for some of the most ambitious consumer brands on this island. Our clients trust us because the team on the floor, in the office, and on the road all take pride in getting the detail right.

We're a scaling business with a strong operational backbone and plenty of room to grow. If you want to work somewhere that takes fulfilment seriously, without the scale or bureaucracy of a big 3PL, this is a good place to land.

What we look for

Operational pride

You notice the detail. You'd rather scan the bin one more time than ship the wrong SKU. You finish what you start.

Brand empathy

You understand that when we ship a parcel, we're the last mile of someone else's brand. Presentation and consistency matter.

Team first

You back up your colleagues, flag issues early, and help new team members find their feet. We're not heroes, we're a crew.
