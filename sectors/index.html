Precision fulfilment for your most sensitive SKUs.

Health and beauty brands face a double challenge: product fragility and regulatory requirements. We handle both. Batch tracking, expiry monitoring, and FIFO rotation are built into every Health & Beauty operation from day one.

  • Batch & expiry date tracking across every SKU
  • Kitting for gift sets, subscription boxes & bundles
  • Branded tissue, ribbon & insert placement per spec
  • Fragile item packing with void fill to brand standard
