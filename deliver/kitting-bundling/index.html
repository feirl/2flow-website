How We Kit

Three Models. One Warehouse.

Different products and business models need different kitting approaches. We handle all three. And we can switch between them as your range evolves.

Pre-Kitting & Gift Sets

We build your sets in advance and hold them as finished inventory. When an order arrives, the kit ships immediately. No assembly delay, no bottleneck at peak. The right approach for seasonal gift sets, fixed bundles, and any product with predictable demand.

Built to your assembly spec, component by component
Finished kits held as individual SKUs in Mintsoft WMS
Scales to thousands of units in a single seasonal run
Retail drops and DTC orders handled concurrently at peak

Subscription Box Fulfilment

Recurring builds require consistency, not just speed. Every subscriber gets the right SKU combination for their tier, every cycle, on time. We work with your billing platform to automate the order flow and treat each fulfilment cycle as a planned production run rather than a spike.

Integrates with ReCharge and major subscription platforms
Per-tier SKU combinations. Monthly, quarterly, or custom cadence
Component stock forecasting ahead of each billing cycle
Churn and upgrade variants handled without operational change

Multi-SKU & On-Demand Kitting

Some bundles are configurable, promotional, or simply too variable to pre-build. We assemble at the time of order. Pulling components, combining to spec, and dispatching in a single pass. Handles product launches, custom bundles, promotional multipacks, and anything that doesn't fit a fixed template.

The Process

From Components to Customer

Every kitting job follows the same structured path. Whether it's a one-off gift set run or a rolling subscription build.

1
Spec & Setup
We document your kit spec in Mintsoft. Components, quantities, assembly order, and any finishing instructions.
2
Stock Induction
All components arrive, are booked in, and held as individual SKUs until the kitting job is raised.
3
Kitting Run
Each unit is assembled to spec and scanned. Pre-kitted sets are held as finished inventory; on-demand kits flow directly to dispatch.
4
QC & Scan
Finished kits are checked and scanned against the job sheet. Discrepancies are flagged before any unit leaves the floor.
5
Dispatch
Kits ship via your carrier of choice. Tracking updates push back to Shopify automatically.
