The Problem with Social Commerce Fulfilment

TikTok moves fast. Most fulfilment operations don't.

The brands winning on TikTok Shop are the ones whose operations are built for it. Here is what breaks when they are not.

Social commerce orders have zero tolerance for dispatch delays

TikTok Shop seller performance ratings depend on dispatch speed. When your product goes viral, hundreds or thousands of orders land in minutes. If your fulfilment partner is not integrated and ready to process same-day, your ratings suffer. And ratings affect the algorithm that made you go viral in the first place.

Live selling creates unpredictable order spikes

A live selling event can move a week's worth of stock in two hours. A regular 3PL treats it like any other day. You need a fulfilment partner who can flex to live commerce peaks without degraded quality or missed dispatch windows.

The unboxing is part of the TikTok content loop

TikTok buyers share unboxing videos. The packaging experience generates content that drives more sales. A plain brown box does not just disappoint a customer, it breaks a content loop that your marketing team cannot buy back.
