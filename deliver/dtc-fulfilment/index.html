Why DTC Fulfilment Breaks Down

A 3PL that isn't integrated isn't a partner. It's overhead.

When your store and your warehouse aren't connected in real time, every order creates manual work. Every manual step creates risk. And every gap between what your store shows and what your warehouse knows becomes a customer service problem waiting to happen.

Problem 01

Your store and warehouse speak different languages

CSV exports, manual order entry, 24-hour lag between sale and fulfilment. These aren't processes, they're workarounds. Every day you're running on yesterday's data, making stock and dispatch decisions with information that's already out of date.

Problem 02

Tracking doesn't reach your customers automatically

"Where's my order?" is the most expensive question in DTC. When your 3PL sends you a spreadsheet with tracking numbers and you have to upload them manually to Shopify, your customers wait. And some of them don't wait quietly. They email, they leave reviews, they don't reorder.

Problem 03

Inventory is always a day behind your decisions

You run a promotion, a product goes viral, a stockist places a big order. And the number in Shopify is wrong because your warehouse hasn't updated it yet. Overselling, emergency stock checks, refund requests. The gap between your store and your 3PL costs you in ways that don't show up on a single line of the P&L.

How It Works

From Checkout to Doorstep Without the Manual Steps

Once the integration is live, the process runs. You don't move orders, update tracking, or adjust inventory manually. The system does it.

1
Platform Connect
Your Shopify store (or other platform) connected to Mintsoft at onboarding. Live sync configured, per-SKU pack instructions set, inventory mapped and reconciled.
2
Order Received
New order detected in Mintsoft the moment it is placed. Routed to the correct pick station with your pack spec applied automatically. No manual intervention.
3
Pick, Pack & Label
Order picked to your per-SKU spec. Branded packaging, inserts, and tissue applied. Carrier label generated at the correct rate for the destination. Quality checked.
4
Dispatch & Track
Handed to carrier before cut-off. Tracking number captured in Mintsoft the moment the carrier scans the parcel. Inventory decremented in real time.
5
Customer Notified
Tracking number pushed back to Shopify automatically. Your customer gets their shipping notification. Your store shows the order as fulfilled. You didn't touch it.
