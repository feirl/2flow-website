What goes into a quote

Your quote is built from six moving parts.

Every 2Flow quote breaks out exactly these components. What drives the cost of each one is visible before you sign anything.

01

Inbound & Receiving

Booking your stock in, checking it against the ASN, putting it away and making it available to pick.

Drivers Shipment frequency, pallet or parcel, complexity of booking-in.
02

Storage

The space your stock takes up on the warehouse floor. Ambient, temperature-controlled or cold chain.

Drivers Bin, shelf or pallet count. Temperature zone. SKU count.
03

Pick & Pack

Picking each order from the shelf, packing it into the right box, generating the label and staging for dispatch.

Drivers Orders per month, lines per order, packaging specification.
04

Value-Add Services

Anything beyond pick and pack. Kitting, engraving, personalisation, gift wrapping, branded inserts.

Drivers What you add to the menu. Priced per unit, per order or per run.
05

Shipping

Getting the parcel from the warehouse to the customer. Domestic, UK, EU and rest of world.

Drivers Carrier mix, destination, weight band, service level.
06

Returns

Processing inbound returns. Inspection, grading, restocking, or refurbishment where needed.

Drivers Return volume, level of inspection, refurbishment requirements.
