FAQs

Common questions about 2Flow

What is 2Flow?

2Flow is Ireland's specialist eCommerce fulfilment partner, based in Dublin. We take full ownership of the operational chain: from inbound stock through to delivered order and returns, so your brand can scale without managing warehouse operations.

What is the cut-off for same-day dispatch?

Same-day dispatch cut-off is 1pm. Orders received before that are dispatched the same working day. For Same-Day Dublin Delivery via our partner Cyclone Couriers, the cut-off is noon with delivery the same afternoon.

Does 2Flow integrate with Shopify?

Yes. We have a native Shopify integration through Mintsoft WMS with instant order sync, automatic tracking updates and real-time inventory visibility. We also integrate with WooCommerce, Amazon, TikTok Shop, ReCharge and other platforms.

Does 2Flow handle subscription brands?

Yes, and we are specifically optimised for them. We integrate with ReCharge, plan our operation around your billing cycle, handle batch kitting with scan-verified assembly, and have a defined process for late-recovered failed payment orders.

Can 2Flow handle temperature-sensitive products?

Yes. We have refrigerated storage on-site for cold-chain SKUs. Same-day Dublin delivery via Cyclone Couriers is also temperature-controlled, currently used for medical products including weight-loss medications shipped in ice packs.

What order volumes does 2Flow work with?

2Flow works best with brands shipping from around 500 orders per month upwards. We have clients ranging from fast-growing DTC brands to established names shipping 10,000+ orders per month across multiple channels.

Does 2Flow handle Amazon FBA preparation?

Yes. 2Flow handles FBA prep including labelling, poly-bagging, carton configuration and compliance. We also do FBM with 24-hour dispatch. 2Flow clients get access to a dedicated Amazon Ireland onboarding programme, including up to 12 months of fee support.

What carriers does 2Flow use?

Our primary carriers are DPD Ireland and An Post for domestic and international shipping. Same-day Dublin delivery is via Cyclone Couriers. Carrier selection is managed by 2Flow. You don't need to manage carrier accounts.
